Privacy Policy for Elite Care co

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

1. Introduction

At Elite Care, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways:

  • Personal Data: Name, shipping address, email address, and telephone number that you voluntarily give to us when you register with our site or express interest in our services.
  • Sensitive Data: Depending on the nature of the care provided, we may collect health information, medical history, or emergency contact details required to provide safe and effective care.
  • Derivative Data: Information our servers automatically collect when you access the site, such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

3. Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you allows us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our care services.
  • Communicate with you regarding appointments, billing, and service updates.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Improve our website and service offerings.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who perform services for us (e.g., payment processing, data analysis, email delivery).
  • Legal Requirements: If we believe the release of information about you is necessary to respond to legal process or protect the rights, property, and safety of others.

5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • The right to access or update the information we have on you.
  • The right to request the deletion of your personal data.
  • The right to opt-out of marketing communications.
